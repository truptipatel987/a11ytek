Accessibility Is Not an Option — It's a Right

Founded by accessibility engineers and disability advocates, A11ytek exists to close the gap between digital products and the 1.3 billion people worldwide living with a disability.

We combine deep technical expertise with lived user experience to deliver accessibility solutions that are real, measurable, and lasting — not checkbox compliance.
